Occupier market momentum slows markedly in Q3

Concerns over the Eurozone recovery and external worries (Ukraine, slower growth in emerging markets) saw occupier market momentum slowing markedly in Q3. Prime office rental growth stalled on aggregate over the quarter and conditions across the region remain very mixed. Nevertheless, prime office rents still stand on aggregate 2.4% higher than a year ago, with only 5 markets showing rents lower today than a year ago.
